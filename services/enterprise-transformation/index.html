Enterprise Transformation

Implementing modern organizational structures and operating models that transcend traditional silos

What We Do?

Citihub helps you transform your enterprise IT operating model. Realizing the full potential of agile methods and cloud-native technologies in a large enterprise, necessitates a holistic approach that encompasses people and processes in addition to technology. The organizational structures, siloed skillsets and rigid processes built up over the last couple of decades are direct inhibitors to a fully adaptive, cloud-enabled enterprise.

Positive change has begun with the adoption of Agile and DevOps methodologies. However, to unleash the full benefits of digital transformation, large enterprises must enable significant shifts in responsibilities and capabilities across management, app teams, infrastructure, security and other control functions. Citihub partners with you to transform those silos and chart the course to adopt an operating model for the digital age.

Our Capabilities

Digital operating model

Design and implementation of modern IT operating models, enabling adoption of methodologies such as Agile, DevOps and Cloud. Our approach is rooted in financial services with tremendous depth in app modernization, transformation, data management and on-prem to cloud transitions. Our track record meeting security, compliance and regulatory requirements is unparalleled.

Agile, DevOps & developer experience

With a focus on the developer experience, Citihub enables the seamless adoption of modern delivery methodologies such as Agile and DevOps. We adapt our approach to suit the specific needs of your organization. This includes Agile coaching, DevOps CI/CD tooling and compliant workflow implementations.

Cloud center of excellence

Evolution of the cloud adoption operating model from supporting initial applications to a broader set of use cases.

Digital workplace

Transformation of the customer experience in physical and virtual workplaces with a focus on consumer-like capabilities for mobility and collaboration.

Operational resiliency

Controls assessment and implementation to ensure effective operational resiliency for critical systems as they transform through their digital journey. Citihub has extensive experience with high-volume core platforms and sophisticated business and trading applications.
