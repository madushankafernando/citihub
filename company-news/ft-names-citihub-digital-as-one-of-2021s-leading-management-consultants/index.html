FT Names Citihub Digital as one of 2021’s Leading Management Consultants

Share this News with a friend.

Thank you for sharing.

related tags

  • awards
  • financial times
  • leading management consultants
  • News

For the fourth year running, The Financial Times has recognized Citihub Digital as one of the UK’s leading management consultants for Financial Institutions and Services for 2021. This award is based on two surveys run by independent data company Statista – one is based on feedback from clients and the other is based on evaluations given by consultants about their peers.

2020 saw two major changes at Citihub. The first was the rebranding of Citihub Consulting as Citihub Digital, a move that represented the firm’s alignment with the evolving consulting needs of its clients. The second was the acquisition of Citihub Digital by Synechron, combining the capabilities of both firms in innovation-led digital transformation, app modernization, cloud enablement, cybersecurity and operating model transformation.

Managing Director Chris Allison comments, “We’re thankful to be recognized for the 4th successive year for our leadership in financial services technology strategy and cloud consulting. “
