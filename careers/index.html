Careers

Work on ground breaking projects at the world’s leading companies

We partner with leading cloud providers and financial services firms to modernize some of the most complex legacy platforms on the planet.

You’ll help our clients transform, remain competitive and relevant; now and into the future.

Working across a broad range of technologies, you will solve complex problems and learn a lot. You will work with the best and be supported by smart, collaborative people who want to see you succeed.

What we’re looking for

We are looking for sharp, curious minds with the energy and drive to be an agent of change for our clients. While there’s no such thing as a typical Citihubber, we do have a few things in common:

  • Tenacity and problem-solving skills
  • The ability to work well under pressure
  • Empathy and sensitivity
  • A positive, can-do mentality
  • A down-to-earth attitude
